Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of users interacting with our Discord bot.


Data Collection

Our bot collects the following data from users:

  • User Name: The name displayed on Discord.
  • User ID: The unique identifier assigned to each user by Discord.

Use of Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to enhance the user experience and to ensure the proper functioning of the bot's features and systems. This may include personalizing responses, tracking interactions, and maintaining functionality.


Data Protection

We take the protection of your data seriously. All collected data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties except when required for operational purposes or by law.


Data Access and Control

As a user, you have the right to access, modify, or delete your data stored by the bot. If you have any concerns or requests regarding your data, please contact us directly via our support server.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, with the updated date at the top of the page. We encourage users to review this policy periodically.


Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out to us through our support server on Discord.
